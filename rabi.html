Rabi Crops

Wheat

Wheat is one of the world's most important staple crops. It belongs to the grass family and is grown mainly for its grain, which is used to produce flour for bread, pasta, pastries, and many other food products. Wheat grains are also used to produce cereals, and its by-products are used as livestock feed.

Barley

Barley is a versatile and ancient cereal grain that belongs to the grass family. It is one of the earliest domesticated crops and has been a staple food for humans and animals for thousands of years. Barley is known for its adaptability to different climates and soil types.

Mustard

Mustard is a versatile plant that belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other crops like cabbage, broccoli, and cauliflower. It is grown primarily for its seeds, which are used to produce mustard condiments, and for its leaves, which can be consumed as greens.

Gram(Chickpeas)

Chickpeas (Cicer arietinum) are a type of legume that is widely cultivated for its nutritious seeds. They are a staple in many cuisines around the world and are known for their versatility and health benefits.
