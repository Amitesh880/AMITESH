Perennial Crops

Coffee

Coffee plants, grown in tropical regions, produce coffee beans. These beans are a major global commodity. The plants can yield beans for several decades. Coffee is known for its stimulating properties due to caffeine. It's a vital part of many cultures and economies.

Tea

Tea plants thrive in subtropical and tropical regions. The leaves are harvested to make various types of tea. These plants can remain productive for many years. Tea is enjoyed worldwide and has numerous health benefits. It plays a significant role in social rituals and traditions.

Banana

Banana plants are cultivated in tropical regions. They continuously produce bananas, a staple fruit, for several years. Bananas are rich in potassium and other nutrients. The plants have large leaves and need warm climates. They are an essential crop for many economies.

Sugarcane

Sugarcane is grown in tropical and subtropical regions. It's used to produce sugar and various by-products. The plant can be harvested multiple times before needing replanting. Sugarcane is crucial for the sugar industry. It also supports biofuel production.
