Zaid Crops

Watermelon

Watermelons are commonly grown during the Zaid season due to the warm temperatures and longer daylight hours. They require ample water and thrive in sandy loam soil.

Cucumber

Cucumbers grow well in warm weather and need well-drained soil. They are commonly used in salads, pickles, and as a refreshing snack. Cucumbers have high water content and are low in calories. They provide vitamins K and C. Cucumbers help keep you hydrated.

Muskmelon (Cantaloupe)

Muskmelons require warm temperatures and adequate irrigation. They produce sweet, juicy, and aromatic fruits. Muskmelons are a good source of vitamins A and C. They are often consumed fresh or in fruit salads. Muskmelons are enjoyed as a healthy summer treat.

Pumpkin

Pumpkins thrive in warm weather and need well-drained soil. They are used in various culinary dishes, from soups to desserts. Pumpkins are rich in vitamins A and C, as well as fiber. They are often carved into jack-o'-lanterns for Halloween. Pumpkin seeds are also a nutritious snack..
